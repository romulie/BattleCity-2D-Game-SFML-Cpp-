C++ and SFML 2.5

"Battle City"-like game. 
Control 2 tanks simultaniously  and shoot enemies!

Features:
- 2-tanks paralel-control gameplay
- Tanks animation: movement, rotation, tracks movement, shooting
- Music and shooting sounds
- Game time and player score counting and displaying
- Interaction with map objects(solid walls, water, bushes (hide players and enemies), pickable items)
- Enemies animation (random respawn, movement and shooting)
- destroying of brick and concrete walls(after picking up GUN)
- Camera zoom (Z/X keys)
